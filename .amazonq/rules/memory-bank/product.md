# Product Overview - Al-Saada ERP Telegram Bot

## Project Purpose

Al-Saada ERP Telegram Bot is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning system delivered through Telegram, designed specifically for Al-Saada Company. It provides a complete business management solution accessible via Telegram's messaging platform, enabling real-time workforce management, inventory control, and financial operations.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Accessible ERP**: Full-featured ERP system accessible through Telegram on any device
- **Arabic-First Design**: Native Arabic interface with RTL support and localized number formatting
- **Real-Time Operations**: Instant updates and notifications for critical business events
- **Role-Based Access**: Granular permissions system ensuring secure data access
- **Integrated Workflows**: Seamless integration between HR, inventory, and financial modules

## Key Features

### Human Resources Management
- **Employee Lifecycle**: Complete employee management from onboarding to termination
- **Attendance & Leave**: Work-leave cycle tracking, leave requests, and mission management
- **Payroll System**: Automated salary calculations with allowances, deductions, and penalties
- **Performance Tracking**: Suspension system, penalty management, and employee history
- **Department Management**: Hierarchical department structure with position assignments

### Inventory Management
- **Multi-Warehouse Support**: Separate systems for oils/greases and spare parts
- **Transaction Management**: Purchase, issue, transfer, return, and adjustment operations
- **Barcode Integration**: QR code scanning for quick item identification
- **Stock Tracking**: Real-time quantity monitoring with low-stock alerts
- **Audit Trail**: Complete history of all inventory movements

### Financial Management
- **Transaction Recording**: Comprehensive financial transaction logging
- **Payroll Integration**: Direct connection between HR and financial systems
- **Advance & Withdrawal**: Employee advance payments and withdrawal tracking
- **Reporting**: Financial summaries and analytics

### Notification System
- **Template-Based**: Customizable notification templates for different events
- **Multi-Channel**: In-app notifications with optional external integrations
- **User Preferences**: Configurable notification settings per user
- **Scheduled Delivery**: Time-based notification scheduling

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Real-Time Dashboards**: Live statistics and KPIs
- **Excel Exports**: Comprehensive data export capabilities
- **Custom Reports**: Flexible reporting system for various business needs
- **Performance Metrics**: Employee, inventory, and financial analytics

## Target Users

### Primary Users
- **Company Administrators**: Full system access and configuration
- **HR Managers**: Employee management and payroll operations
- **Inventory Managers**: Stock control and warehouse operations
- **Department Heads**: Team oversight and approval workflows
- **Employees**: Self-service access to personal information and requests

### User Roles
- **OWNER**: Complete system control
- **ADMIN**: Administrative operations
- **MANAGER**: Department and team management
- **EMPLOYEE**: Basic access to personal features
- **GUEST**: Limited read-only access
